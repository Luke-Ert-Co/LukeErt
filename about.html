About Me

Hi, I'm Luke. I'm a designer and student based in England. I specialize in creating grungy and interesting graphics and designs for businesses and individuals.

I am currently studying design and animation, where I am constantly learning and refining my skills in various areas of graphic design. From logo and branding design to print and digital media, I am always eager to take on new challenges and expand my knowledge.

One aspect of design that particularly fascinates me is the power of storytelling through visuals. I believe that every design has the potential to convey a narrative and evoke emotions. Whether it's through the careful selection of colors, the arrangement of elements, or the integration of typography, I strive to create designs that not only catch the eye but also communicate a deeper message.

Within my work I combine both physical and digital design via use of a photo scanner and a typewriter. Some of my work contains warped scanned pages of books or scanned items for use as a texture. I also am interested in film photography, with some of my photos being scanned and used within my work, a roll of my film has been used to create the border of this website.
